How Barly Works

Trade skills. Learn together. Grow faster.

1. Create Your Profile

Tell us what you want to learn and what you can teach. Whether it’s web design or photography, your profile is your gateway to new connections.

2. Get Matched

We match you with someone who wants to learn your skill and can teach you theirs. No fees — just people helping people grow.

3. Schedule a Session

Coordinate with your match and decide when and how to meet. Whether it's Zoom or a coffee shop, learning should fit your lifestyle.

4. Track & Grow

After each exchange, leave feedback and keep track of your progress. Barly helps you build your learning journey one skill at a time.
